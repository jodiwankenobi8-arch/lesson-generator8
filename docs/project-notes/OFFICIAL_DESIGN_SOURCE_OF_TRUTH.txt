==================================================
6A. TEXTURE + SCRAPBOOK DIRECTION
==================================================

Additional style rule:
The app should feel like a classy scrapbook with layered textures.

Meaning:
- tactile
- curated
- warm
- elegant
- slightly handmade in a refined way
- varied in surface feel, but still cohesive

This does NOT mean:
- crafty-chaotic
- junky collage
- messy patchwork
- childish scrapbook stickers everywhere
- visual clutter
- cheap decorative overload

Approved texture direction:
- subtle paper/canvas grain
- lightly painted or soft printed feel
- layered surfaces that feel collected and intentional
- quiet variation between page background, cards, ribbons, and accent panels
- gentle contrast in texture rather than loud contrast in color

Implementation guidance:
- texture should stay subtle and low-contrast
- texture should support the teacher-first calm mood
- texture should make the interface feel human and tactile, not noisy
- different surfaces can feel slightly different, but all must remain polished and coordinated
- if a texture feels kitschy, busy, crafty, or “riffraff,” it is wrong

Decision rule:
Prefer “curated elegant scrapbook” over both:
1. sterile SaaS flatness
2. overly decorated crafty clutter
