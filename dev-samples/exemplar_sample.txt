EXEMPLAR TYPE: GUIDEPOST / CLICKABLE TOC / SECTION NAV
SOURCE: Teacher-built model lesson (structure only)

[STYLE TOKENS]
- Theme: Calm blues + white background
- Layout: Title top-left, agenda icons in circles, consistent footer buttons
- Buttons: Home | TOC | Next | Back
- Navigation: Clickable circles TOC; selected section in color, others gray
- Timers: appear on practice slides as "Timer: 60s" etc.
- Presenter: uses clicker; "Clicker: next" prompts exist

[GLOBAL SLIDE RULES]
- Every section starts with a SECTION MENU slide:
  - shows all circles (Songs, Phonics/UFLI, Read Aloud, Turn & Talk, Practice, Exit)
  - current section is COLOR; others are GRAY
  - has Home + TOC buttons
- Sections must be skippable via TOC at any time
- Keep student-facing text short; teacher notes in speaker notes only (if present)

[TOC CIRCLES]
1) SONGS
2) UFLI / PHONICS
3) READ ALOUD
4) TURN & TALK
5) PRACTICE
6) EXIT

- Note: Clicker instruction: "Clicker: next after verse 1"

[SECTION: UFLI / PHONICS]

[SECTION: PRACTICE]
